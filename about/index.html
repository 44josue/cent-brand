Our Story

Born from the streets of Kigali

CENT started with a simple idea: Rwandan youth deserved clothing that reflected their identity — bold, honest, and built to last. Not imported aesthetics slapped on local bodies, but a genuine expression of where we come from and where we're going.

Every design, every stitch, every drop is a statement. We move with purpose, and we price with integrity. Every cent matters — in the details of a garment and in the value we deliver to our community.

The Mission

Authentic. Affordable. Unapologetic.

We don't chase trends — we set them. CENT operates on limited drops, intentional designs, and direct relationships with our community. No middlemen inflating prices. No compromises on quality.

Payments through MTN MoMo and Airtel Money keep everything local and accessible. Delivery across Rwanda. Community first, always.
